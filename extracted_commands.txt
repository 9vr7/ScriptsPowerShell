COPIAR FICHEROS(LINUX)

1. ls -l
   - List detailed contents of the current directory.

2. ls dir3
   - List contents of the 'dir3' directory.

3. ls carpetacopiada01
   - List contents of the 'carpetacopiada01' directory.

4. ls -l carpetacopiada01
   - List detailed contents of the 'carpetacopiada01' directory.

5. cp -v carpetacopiada01/f*.txt dir3
   - Copy all files starting with 'f' from 'carpetacopiada01' to 'dir3', with verbose output.

6. ls -l dir3
   - List detailed contents of the 'dir3' directory after copying files.

7. ls -al dir3
   - List all contents, including hidden files, of 'dir3' with detailed information.
